WaterSurface model

The mesh is displaced on the GPU by gerstnerDisplace/gerstnerNormal. Each buoy is positioned on the CPU by GerstnerSurface.getHeight/getNormal from the same resolved wave list and the same elapsed time. If they ever drift apart, a buoy will visibly float above or sink below the surface.
